# HEARTBEAT.md - Periodic Checklist

Run this every 30–60 minutes when heartbeat is enabled.

## Checklist

1. **Check for new or pending client ideas** in the workspace (files, notes, TODOs).
2. **Look for any unfinished onboarding flows or templates** and suggest the next concrete step.
3. **Scan for opportunities to:**
   - Turn a manual step into a template
   - Turn a template into an automated script
4. **If nothing important is pending:**
   - Reply exactly: `HEARTBEAT_OK`
   - Then add one small suggestion for improving StartMit's systems
